Company Profile

Zhejiang Yokey Intelligent Manufacturing Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2025, is a technology-driven company specializing in the research and development of core technologies for embodied intelligent power systems and high-end intelligent manufacturing. With its manufacturing headquarters located in Yueqing, Zhejiang, and its R&D base in Beijing, the company has established a dual-core strategic layout.

The Yueqing manufacturing headquarters is deeply integrated with and collaboratively developed alongside Taibang Motor Industry Group, which has more than 30 years of experience in the geared motor industry. Leveraging Taibang Group’s mature manufacturing system, industrial expertise, and supply chain advantages in precision transmission and geared motors, Yokeyrobo is supported by strong capabilities in large-scale production and quality assurance.

Supported by a mature manufacturing system, the company brings together experts from renowned academic institutions and the embodied intelligence industry to continuously drive technological iteration and build a full-chain ecosystem integrating R&D, production, sales, and service. The company has achieved full-stack independent R&D and mass production of core components including planetary reducers, harmonic reducers, cycloidal reducers, servo motors, frameless motors, control drivers, and integrated joint modules. Its products are widely applied in humanoid robots, quadruped robots, collaborative robotic arms, electric grippers, commercial aerospace, low-altitude economy, marine engineering, and other fields.

Based on its advantages in self-developed core components and large-scale manufacturing, the company is further expanding into the R&D and industrialization of quadruped robots and humanoid robot systems. With strong technological barriers and full industrial chain capabilities, Yokeyrobo is committed to becoming an industry benchmark in the field of core components and complete robot systems for embodied intelligence.
